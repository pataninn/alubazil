Our Goals

Initial Phase: We want to connect to the people and make the AlubaZil known as an eco-friendly solution to purify water. AlubaZil will be sold to target customers, like hikers, campers, and adventurers, and crowdfunding investment will be the main source of funds alongside with partnerships with environmental organizations. AlubaZil will be known to the public as a safe, clean, and green water purifier.

Next Phase: We aim to reach the people in rural areas that lack access to clean and safe water. With the help of organizations, such as the UN, we can distribute AlubaZil to people in need. AlubaZil will also shift from in-house production to selling manufacturing rights to local manufacturers. Moreover, 20% of our profit will be dedicated to assisting our partnered organizations in distributing AlubaZil.

All in all, we will work towards reaching people with water problems by partnering with organizations and using money from sales profit and crowdfunding.
