Our Team

Our team consists of 5 high schoolers and 1 chemistry teacher from Kamnoetvidya Science Academy (KVIS). Our vibrant team consist of Patanin, Nattakorn, Worachon, Chananrat, Rawisara and our coach Yutichai. Our love for chemistry and the environment has brought us together to discover AluBaZil. We all share an interest in chemistry and want to use their knowledge to create something innovative that can help the people and the world. We sat down together and come up with a great problem to solve, water scarcity. Water is essential for every single life on Earth. Without water, people would not be able to live their life like people who can access clean and safe water. A solution is to make unusable water clean and safe to use on site, hence the creation of AlubaZil.
